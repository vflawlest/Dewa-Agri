About Dewa Agri

Dewa Agri is an integrated coconut processing company based in Indonesia. We focus on developing sustainable plantations and transforming coconuts into high-value products.

Our operations combine plantation development, modern processing, and strict quality control to ensure reliable coconut products for domestic and international markets.
